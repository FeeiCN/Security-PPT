Tech Foundations
Place your workforce on a path to tech fluency

To drive innovation and successfully compete, your organization needs to understand the key technologies impacting your business. Tech Foundations offers a simple way for anyone, regardless of technical background, to become conversant in those key technologies.
By empowering your workforce to correctly apply tech concepts to the business, your org will decrease knowledge silos, identify technology opportunities and increase cross-functional collaboration for faster innovation.

What is Tech Foundations? Tech Foundations is a user-friendly program that helps learners gain a stronger understanding of 12 key tech concepts through expert-led courses, pre- and postassessments, in-app analytics, and an easy to navigate user interface. Tech Foundations can help you:
>> Decrease knowledge silos Enable learners to understand how key tech concepts impact different teams across the business with engaging, approachable courses taught by Pluralsight experts. Widespread knowledge fosters better collaboration and decision-making.
>> Identify opportunities for technology Empower learners to understand how to apply key tech concepts, why they matter and how they work with other technologies in an organization's stack. Identifying both synergies between technologies and opportunities for new technology opens the door to innovation.
>> Track achievement and progress at scale Tech Foundation's analytics and preand post-assessments help managers understand how their learners are tracking toward fluency in key tech topics.
