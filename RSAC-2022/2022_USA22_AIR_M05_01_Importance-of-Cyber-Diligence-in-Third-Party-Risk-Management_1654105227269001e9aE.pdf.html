Identify which vendors, how they have access, and what they need in order to prioritize security requirements of your full vendor lists. Verify Security and Repeat Risk Audits Periodically. Do not rely on hearsay of cyber hygiene of crucial vendors and check on them to ensure continued diligence. Ask Questions.
